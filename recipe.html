æbleskivers Recipe

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 75 min
Serves: 24 aebleskivers

1) Gather Supplies

Ensure you have a gas stove, an æbelsekiver pan(preheat to 400 degrees aka medium-high), bowls, measuring spoons, sifter, and a standing mixer before starting.

2) Prep Ingredients

Gather together the ingredients which are: flour, salt, granulated sugar, nutmeg, eggs, buttermilk, regular milk, Crisco, butter, and baking soda.

3) Dry + Wet Ingredients

Dry: Into a large bowl sift together the flour, salt, sugar, and then nutmeg.

Wet: In another bowl whisk egg yolks, buttermilk, and milk. Add in after the dissolved basking soda+warm water mixture.

In another bowl (using standing mixer) beat egg whites until soft peaks are formed.

4) Combine + Egg Whites

Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients. Stir until no dry streaks remain (a few small lumps are ok). Do not over mix.

Gently fold in 1/2 of beaten egg whites into the batter.

5) Cook

Combine melted butter anc Crisco and then use it to scoop 1 tbs of the mixture into each circle of pan. Turn heat down to 300 degrees before adding batter. Fill the half circles until 3/4 full. When edges are cooked enough to move, use a skewer to turn each ball a quarter at a time until golden. Adjust heat back to 350 degrees between batches.

6) Last Half of Batter

Fold in the second half of the beaten egg whites to the last half of the batter, to maintain fluff. Then Finish cooking the rest of the batter.

7) Serve

Remove and dust with powdered sugar. Serve warm with jam, syrup, Cinnamon sugar, or Nutella and Fruit!

Enjoy!! :)
