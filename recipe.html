æbleskivers Recipe

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 90 min
Serves: 24

1) Gather & Prep Ingredients

Measure dry ingredients; separate eggs; melt butter.

2) Mix Dry Ingredients

Whisk flour, sugar, salt, and baking soda together.

3) Heat Pan

Preheat æbleskiver pan over medium-high; lightly butter cups.

Ingredients

  • Flour2 cups
  • Salt1/2 tsp
  • Sugar2 tbsp
  • Egg yolks4
  • Buttermilk1 ⅔ cups
  • Baking soda1/2 tsp
  • Butter (melted)½ stick
